Pattern Syntax:

  • drums: kick, snare, hihat, clap (use '-' for rest)
  • bass: Note names (C2, E2, G2, etc.) or '-' for rest
  • synth: Note names (C4, E4, G4, etc.) or '-' for rest

Example:

drums: kick snare kick snare
bass: C2 - E2 - G2 - E2
synth: C4 E4 G4 E4 C4 E4 G4 E4
